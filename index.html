Home

In 1972 when the school was new, children walked with tiny, apprehensive steps into the E-47 Anand Niketan address, their first day of formal education marked the birth of Mount Carmel School. The journey has taken the founders across the globe and the school into the lives of thousands of citizens.Today, Mount Carmel is recognized as one of the premier educational institutions in the country, dedicated to the cause of making a positive difference in the lives of our future leaders, striving to uphold and enhance integrity, discipline, hard work and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
